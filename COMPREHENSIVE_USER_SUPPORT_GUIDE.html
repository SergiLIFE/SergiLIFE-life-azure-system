🛠️ Troubleshooting Made Simple

Click on any problem below for a quick solution. Still stuck? Our support team is here to help!

🔌 My EEG device won't connect

Quick Fix:

  1. Check USB cable connection or Bluetooth pairing
  2. Restart the L.I.F.E. app and your device
  3. Update device drivers (we'll guide you)
  4. Try a different USB port or reset Bluetooth

Still not working? Contact support with your device model - we'll fix it within 1 hour!

🔑 Login trouble or forgot password

Reset Password:

  1. Go to lifecoach-121.com/login
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Check your email (including spam folder)
  4. Follow the reset link

Account locked? This happens after 5 failed attempts. Wait 15 minutes or contact support for immediate unlock.

🐌 App running slowly or freezing

Speed It Up:

  1. Close other programs (especially video streaming)
  2. Check internet speed (need 10+ Mbps)
  3. Clear browser cache or restart mobile app
  4. Switch to nearest server location in settings

System Requirements: Windows 10+, 8GB RAM, decent internet. Not meeting these? Contact us for optimization tips!

📊 Data not syncing or missing progress

Sync Recovery:

  1. Check internet connection
  2. Log out and log back in
  3. Force sync from settings menu
  4. Contact support - we backup everything!

Data Recovery: We keep 90 days of backups. Your progress is never truly lost!

💳 Billing or subscription issues

Common Solutions:

  1. Check card expiration date
  2. Verify billing address matches bank records
  3. Try a different payment method
  4. Contact support for manual processing

Need help? Our billing team responds within 2 hours and can set up payment plans if needed.

🔊 Audio not working during sessions

Audio Fix:

  1. Check system volume and L.I.F.E. app volume
  2. Test with headphones vs speakers
  3. Update audio drivers
  4. Try different audio output device

Pro Tip: Headphones work best for neuroadaptive audio features!
