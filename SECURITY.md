Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability in the L.I.F.E. Platform, please report it responsibly and confidentially to our dedicated security team.

How to Report
Email: Sergi@lifecoach-121.com
Subject Line: [SECURITY] - L.I.F.E Platform Vulnerability Report
Include:

A clear description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.

Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable).

Affected version(s) of the platform.

Your recommended remediation (if available).

Your contact information for follow-up correspondence.

Reporting Expectations
Initial Response Time: 24–48 hours. We will confirm receipt of your report and begin initial triage.

Assessment Period: 5–7 business days. Our security team will investigate, assess severity, and determine remediation timeline.

Update Cadence: Weekly status updates will be provided for any critical vulnerabilities.

Timeline to Resolution:

Critical (CVSS 9–10): Patch released within 72 hours; urgent notification sent to all support
