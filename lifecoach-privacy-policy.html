1. Information We Collect

L.I.F.E. Platform collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email, organization details
  • Usage Data: Learning sessions, performance metrics, system interactions
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, browser details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process EEG signals for neuroadaptive learning optimization
  • Provide personalized learning recommendations
  • Generate performance analytics and progress reports
  • Ensure platform security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations and industry regulations

3. Data Security and Protection

Encryption and Storage

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and in transit
  • Azure Infrastructure: Secure cloud storage with enterprise-grade protection
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection

4. Compliance Standards

Healthcare (HIPAA)

  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA) available for covered entities
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) safeguards
  • Audit trails for all data access and modifications
  • Breach notification procedures in place

Education (FERPA)

  • Student educational record protection
  • Parental consent mechanisms for minors
  • Directory information handling procedures
  • Data use agreements with educational institutions

European Union (GDPR)

  • Lawful basis for processing personal data
  • Data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
  • Privacy impact assessments for high-risk processing
  • Data Protection Officer contact available
