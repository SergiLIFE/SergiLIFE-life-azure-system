Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 8, 2025

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

1. Introduction

L.I.F.E. Platform ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our neuroadaptive learning platform and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Billing and payment information
  • Usage data and platform interactions

2.2 EEG and Biometric Data

  • Electroencephalogram (EEG) signals and measurements
  • Neural response patterns and brain wave data
  • Learning performance metrics and cognitive assessments
  • Anonymized aggregated data for platform improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide real-time neuroadaptive learning services
  • Process EEG data for personalized learning optimization
  • Generate analytics and performance reports
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Ensure compliance with healthcare and education regulations
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

4. Data Protection and Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • End-to-end AES-256 encryption for all data transmission
  • Azure Managed Identity for secure authentication
  • Private endpoints and network security groups
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance framework

4.2 Data Storage

  • All data stored in Microsoft Azure cloud infrastructure
  • Data residency options available for regional compliance
  • Automated backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Zero data retention policy options available

5. Regulatory Compliance

5.1 HIPAA Compliance (Healthcare)

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAA) available
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) safeguards
  • Audit logging and access controls
  • Minimum necessary data access principles

5.2 GDPR Compliance (European Union)

  • Lawful basis for processing personal data
  • Data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure)
  • Data protection impact assessments
  • Privacy by design and default principles

5.3 FERPA Compliance (Education)

  • Educational record protection and privacy
  • Parental consent procedures for minors
  • Directory information handling
  • Student data use restrictions

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights, property, or safety
  • In connection with business transfers (with notice)
  • With trusted service providers under strict agreements

7. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while account is active plus 90 days
  • EEG data: Configurable retention periods (24 hours to 7 years)
  • Aggregated analytics: Retained indefinitely in anonymized form
  • Audit logs: Retained for 7 years for compliance purposes

9. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by regulatory authorities
  • Microsoft Azure global compliance certifications

10. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential cookies for platform functionality
  • Analytics cookies for usage optimization (opt-in)
  • No third-party advertising or tracking cookies
  • Cookie preferences can be managed in account settings

11. Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA and similar regulations. For users under 13:

  • Parental consent required before data collection
  • Limited data collection and use
  • Enhanced privacy protections
  • Parents can review and request deletion of child's data
